About Tomalistis Falls

Tomalistis Falls is a hidden gem located in Brgy. Tomalistis, Caibiran, Biliran. Tucked within lush greenery and a serene forest setting, the falls cascade gracefully down natural limestone rocks into a clear, cool basin â€” a refreshing sight and experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike. What makes Tomalistis Falls truly unique is its reputation for having one of the cleanest natural spring waters in the Philippines. In fact, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) once recognized its water as among the cleanest inland bodies of water in the country.
